Our philosophy

Made slow.
On purpose.

Canvas Theory started with a simple belief — everything is a canvas if you look hard enough. Each jacket, tote, and clock starts as a plain piece and is hand-painted to your exact spec. We take our time because the work demands it.

You tell us what you want. We make it exactly that way. Then we send you photos as it comes to life — so you watch it being made.

yours to define."
