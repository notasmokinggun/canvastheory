Our story

Made by hand.
Made for you.

Canvas Theory hand-paints jackets, totes, and clocks in Bangalore — to your exact spec, never anyone else's.

How it started

A blank jacket
and a bad idea.

Canvas Theory started in 2025 with one plain denim jacket and a paintbrush. The first piece was for a friend — hand-painted, freehand, no stencils, learning the surface as it went. She wore it constantly.

That was the moment the idea stopped being a favor and started being a question: what if everyday things — jackets, totes, even a clock on the wall — were treated as a canvas instead of just an object?

"She wore it constantly. That was the moment it stopped being a favor."

Materials

Plain pieces.
Painted by hand.

Every piece starts as a plain blue denim jacket, a plain canvas tote, or a plain quartz wall clock — sourced as-is and hand-painted from there. We're not a denim manufacturer; we're painters working on a ready-made surface, so what you're paying for is the design, not the construction.
